Let’s see if your device is compatible for YO. The fastest way is to tell us your device’s IMEI number. It indicates your device’s manufacturer, model, and supported frequencies.

Your device needs to be unlocked to be compatible for YO.

How do I find my IMEI?

On an Apple phone:
Go to Settings > General > About. Your phone ID (IMEI) is about 3/4 of the way down.

On an Android phone:
Go to Settings > About Phone. Your phone ID (IMEI) should be one of the first things you see.

Note: Compatibility check does not guarantee your device will work on our network.

Need more help? Contact an agent using the (?) button in the bottom right.
